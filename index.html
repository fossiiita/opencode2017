About OPENCODE

FOSS IIITA is an open source organization under Geekhaven of IIIT Allahabad.

Opencode is a month-long open source event starting on January 17, 2017, in which all undergraduates and postgraduates around the world can participate in developing open sourced projects and contributing in them. Opencode aims to raise awareness about open source software development among the students and developers around the world.
